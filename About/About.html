Mastering UI/UX Design: My Journey

I am currently a Master's student majoring in Information Science at Cornell University. My studies cover everything from aesthetical user interface design to user experience and behavior psychology. Through hands-on projects and real-world exposure, I've honed my skills in crafting intuitive and visually appealing interfaces.

My master's program equips me to bridge the gap between user needs and technology capabilities, making me a valuable asset in the world of UI/UX design. I'm excited to shape the digital experiences of the future. Explore my portfolio to see my journey unfold.
