About Boris Hu

Think Broadly. Design Inclusively.

I am currently a student majoring in Human-Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle. Suffering from ankylosing spondylitis, I pay special attention to disabilities and accessibility facilities and services in my designs. I seek to design solutions for people in marginalized communities and create satisfying experiences that could help them to increase convenience in their lives.

With these expectations, I would like to learn more innovative design and engineering skills as well as practical abilities to help me to achieve my ambitions.
